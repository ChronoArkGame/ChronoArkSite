FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play Chrono Ark with my friends in co-op mode?

Yes! Chrono Ark supports multiplayer co-op, allowing you to team up with your friends to battle through randomized dungeons and defeat powerful enemies together.

How does the multiplayer system work?

In multiplayer mode, each player controls a different character in the party. You’ll need to coordinate your skills and actions to survive challenging battles and progress through the game.

Is there PvP mode in Chrono Ark?

Currently, Chrono Ark focuses on cooperative gameplay. However, a competitive PvP mode is under development, where players will be able to test their deck-building strategies against each other.

Does Chrono Ark have cross-platform multiplayer?

Yes, Chrono Ark supports cross-platform play, so you can team up with friends regardless of whether they are playing on PC or other supported platforms.

How do I invite friends to my game?

You can invite friends through the in-game lobby system. Simply create a room, share the invite code, and they can join your party seamlessly.

What happens if a player disconnects during a run?

If a player disconnects, the AI will temporarily take over their character until they reconnect. If they fail to return, the remaining players can continue the adventure.

Are there multiplayer-exclusive features?

Yes! Multiplayer mode includes unique synergy mechanics, special co-op events, and boss fights that require teamwork to overcome. These features make multiplayer gameplay a fresh and exciting experience!
